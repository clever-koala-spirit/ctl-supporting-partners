Supporting Partners

Brands that make free recycling possible

Close the Loop operates manufacturer-funded product stewardship programs in partnership with leading global brands across Australia, New Zealand and internationally.

For more than 25 years, Close the Loop has supported manufacturers, retailers and brand owners to take responsibility for their products at end of life — designing recovery systems that are practical, compliant and genuinely circular.

These programs span printer consumables, cosmetics and other complex product categories where responsible recovery requires specialist infrastructure, logistics and reporting.

How supporting partners make the program free

Close the Loop’s recycling programs are made possible by a group of supporting brands who voluntarily fund product stewardship on behalf of their customers.

These partners cover the real costs of:

Collection and logistics

Recycling and material recovery

Program operations, reporting and compliance

Because of this commitment, customers using supported brands can recycle at no cost.

This funding supports long-term, responsible recovery outcomes — including local material reuse initiatives such as TonerPlas, which transforms recovered materials into new infrastructure applications. Learn more about TonerPlas

Planet Ark and Cartridges 4 Planet Ark

Close the Loop proudly operates the Cartridges 4 Planet Ark program in partnership with Planet Ark.

This multi-vendor program provides over 3,800 public drop-off locations across Australia and is designed for people who do not have access to workplace collections.

This option is ideal for:

Home users

Small offices and community groups

Anyone with occasional cartridge returns

If you use cartridges from any supported print partner, you can recycle them at the participating public drop-off locations below at no cost.

Please note: At some locations, collection boxes may be stored behind the counter due to space constraints. Ask staff if you cannot see the box.

Cosmetics Partners

Close the Loop works with cosmetics and personal care brands that fund the recovery of hard-to-recycle packaging on behalf of their customers.

These brands voluntarily support:

  • In-store and public collection
  • Sorting and specialised recycling
  • Responsible end-of-life outcomes for cosmetic packaging

This support helps keep packaging out of landfill while improving transparency and recovery across the supply chain.
